COVID-19

Overview

Coronavirus , the name of a family of virus. coronavirus belongs to a large family of virus like MARS and SRAS. the last one being COVID-19 is a respiratory illness the official name of the pandemia. The new coronavirus (Covid-19) has spread to nearly every country in the world since it first emerged in China at the beginning of the year. Confirmed cases pass 3.5 million worldwide, according to Johns Hopkins University. Almost 250,000 people have died and 1.1 million have recovered.Symptoms include fever, coughing, sore throat and shortness of breath. The virus can spread from person to person.

Lockdown

UK prime misister Mr Johnson lockeddown to all non-essential business and banned travel and a asked people to stay at home. only leave the house for exercise like a walk or a jog, to go work if they are a key worker and to buy essential items such as food or medicine. Police have been given powers to arrest people & issue fines.

Preventions

Wash your hands at least 20 seconds as soon as you get home Do not meet others, even friends or family. You can spread the virus even if you don't have symptoms.If you go out, stay 2 metres (6ft) away from other people at all times .avoid getting infected for people who: are 70 or over,have a long-term condition, pregnant and have a weakened immune system.

If you have covid-19

If you need medical help for any reason, do not go to places like a GP surgery, pharmacy or hospital. If you have symptoms of coronavirus (a high temperature or a new, continuous cough), use the 111 coronavirus service. For life-threatening emergencies, call 999 for an ambulance.
